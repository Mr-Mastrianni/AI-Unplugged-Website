Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up a chatbot?

Basic chatbots can be up and running within 1-2 weeks. More complex, custom solutions may take 3-4 weeks, depending on the level of customization and integration required.

Will the chatbot understand my industry-specific terminology?

Yes! We train each chatbot on your industry-specific terms and business vocabulary. The more you use it, the better it gets at understanding and responding to specialized terminology.

Can the chatbot handle complex customer inquiries?

Our chatbots are designed to handle a wide range of inquiries, from simple FAQs to more complex interactions. For very complex issues, the chatbot can seamlessly transfer the conversation to a human agent while maintaining context.

How do I update the chatbot's knowledge base?

We provide an easy-to-use administrative dashboard where you can update information, add new responses, and refine existing ones. Our team is also available to assist with larger updates or modifications.

Will the chatbot work on mobile devices?

Yes, our chatbots are fully responsive and work seamlessly across all devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
