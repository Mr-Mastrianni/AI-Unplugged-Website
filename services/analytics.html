Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of data do I need to get started?

To begin with predictive analytics, you'll need historical business data such as sales records, customer information, inventory data, or operational metrics. The specific data required depends on your goals, but generally, 12-24 months of historical data provides a solid foundation. We can help you identify and gather the relevant data from your existing systems.

How accurate are the predictions?

Prediction accuracy varies based on several factors, including data quality, historical patterns, and the specific business problem. Our models typically achieve 80-95% accuracy for most business forecasting. We continuously monitor and refine the models over time to maintain and improve accuracy. We also provide confidence intervals with our predictions to help you understand the level of certainty.

Do I need technical expertise to use your analytics platform?

No technical expertise is required. Our platform is designed with user-friendly dashboards and intuitive interfaces that make it easy for business users to access insights and predictions. We provide comprehensive training and ongoing support to ensure you can effectively use all features. The complex technical aspects are handled behind the scenes by our team.

How long does implementation take?

Implementation typically takes 3-6 weeks, depending on the complexity of your business and the scope of the analytics solution. Basic implementations can be completed in as little as 2 weeks, while more complex, enterprise-level solutions may take 8-10 weeks. We work closely with you to establish a clear timeline and implementation plan at the start of the project.

Can your analytics integrate with my existing systems?

Yes, our predictive analytics platform is designed to integrate with most common business systems including CRM platforms, ERP systems, e-commerce platforms, POS systems, and accounting software. We offer pre-built connectors for popular systems like Salesforce, QuickBooks, Shopify, and more. For custom or legacy systems, we can develop specific integrations to ensure seamless data flow.
