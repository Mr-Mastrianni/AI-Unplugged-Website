About AI Unplugged

Making AI accessible and practical for small businesses.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, AI Unplugged emerged from a simple observation: while AI technology was advancing rapidly, small businesses were being left behind due to technical complexity and high costs.

Our founders, a team of AI specialists and small business consultants, recognized this gap and set out to bridge it by creating affordable, easy-to-implement AI solutions specifically designed for small businesses.

Since then, we've been on a mission to democratize AI technology, making it accessible to businesses of all sizes. We believe that AI shouldn't be reserved for big corporations with deep pocketsâ€”it should be a tool that empowers all businesses to grow and thrive in the digital age.

Our Mission

At AI Unplugged, our mission is to empower small businesses by providing them with affordable, easy-to-use AI solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and innovation.

We strive to:

  • Make AI technology accessible to businesses of all sizes
