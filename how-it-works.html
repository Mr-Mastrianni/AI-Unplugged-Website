Our Implementation Process

At AI Unplugged, we've developed a straightforward, efficient process to get you up and running with the right AI solutions for your business. We handle the complexity so you can focus on what matters most—growing your business.

1

Consultation

We start with a free, no-obligation consultation to understand your business, challenges, and goals. Our team listens carefully to identify where AI can make the biggest impact.

What to Expect:

  • > A 30-45 minute discussion with our solution experts
  • > Questions about your current processes and pain points
  • > Exploration of your business goals and objectives
  • > Initial recommendations for AI solutions that may help
2

Customization

Based on our consultation, we design a tailored solution that addresses your specific needs. We configure our AI tools to integrate seamlessly with your existing systems and workflows.

What to Expect:

  • > A detailed proposal outlining recommended solutions
  • > Customization of AI tools to match your business requirements
  • > Integration planning with your existing systems
  • > Timeline and implementation roadmap
3

Implementation

Our technical team handles the setup and deployment of your AI solutions. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your business operations while ensuring everything is properly configured.

What to Expect:

  • > Dedicated implementation manager to oversee the process
  • > Technical setup handled by our expert team
  • > Regular updates on progress and timeline
  • > Initial testing and quality assurance
4

Training

We provide comprehensive training to ensure you and your team can effectively use and manage your new AI tools. Our training is hands-on and focused on practical application in your business context.

What to Expect:

  • > Interactive training sessions for your team
  • > Customized user guides and documentation
  • > Video tutorials for future reference
  • > Q&A sessions to address specific questions
5

Ongoing Support

Our relationship doesn't end with implementation. We provide ongoing support, regular check-ins, and continuous optimization to ensure your AI solutions deliver maximum value over time.

What to Expect:

  • > Dedicated support team available via email, phone, or chat
  • > Regular performance reviews and optimization suggestions
  • > System updates and improvements
  • > Quarterly strategy consultations (depending on your plan)

Implementation Timeline

Most small business AI implementations can be completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity and scope. Here's a typical timeline for getting started with AI Unplugged:

Week 1

  • > Initial consultation
  • > Requirements gathering
  • > Solution design
  • > Proposal and agreement

Week 2

  • > Configuration setup
  • > Integration with existing systems
  • > Initial deployment
  • > Basic testing

Week 3

  • > Comprehensive testing
  • > Refinements and adjustments
  • > Team training sessions
  • > Documentation delivery

Week 4

  • > Full launch
  • > Extended team training
  • > Initial performance review
  • > Support transition

Note: Timeline may vary based on solution complexity and specific business requirements. Some straightforward implementations can be completed in as little as 1-2 weeks.
